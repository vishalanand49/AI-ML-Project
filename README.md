## General Information
BoomBikes, a US-based bike-sharing company, experienced a decline in revenue due to the COVID-19 pandemic. To recover, the company aims to understand the factors affecting bike demand.
The objective of this project is to:
Identify key factors influencing bike rental demand
Build a predictive model to forecast demand (cnt)
Help management optimise operations and improve profitability
Dataset: Daily bike-sharing data (day.csv)
Contains information such as:
Weather conditions (temperature, humidity, windspeed)
Seasonal factors (season, month, year)
User behaviour (working day, holiday)
Target variable: cnt (total rentals)
## Conclusions
Temperature has the strongest positive impact on bike demand, indicating higher rentals in warmer conditions.
Demand increased significantly in 2019 compared to 2018, reflecting growing popularity of bike-sharing services.
Weather conditions play a critical role – clear weather increases usage, while rain or extreme conditions reduce demand.
Seasonality affects demand, with highest usage observed in summer and fall seasons.
